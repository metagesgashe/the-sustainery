EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

OVERVIEW & PROBLEM

Today’s clothing production system is extremely wasteful and polluting. In fact, the apparel industry is the #2 most polluting industry in theworld. Large amounts of non-renewable resources are ulitized to produce textiles and fabric. The post-industrial textile waste ends up in landfills or incinerators. Thefashion industry has a pollution problem and consumers want brands to help themto improve their environmental footprints by offering more sustainable choices.

SOLUTION

The Sustainery is a B2B marketplace offering a solution that supports a circular economy and enables a more effective system for utilizing unwanted,wasted and deadstock textiles. We connect suppliers and sustainable designers through a collaborative effort with unwanted textiles that would otherwise end upin a landfill.
